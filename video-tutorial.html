Video Embedding Tutorial.

Step 1:

Find a video on Youtube and click the share button. This button is located underneath the viewing window, right next to the like and dislike buttons.

Step 2:

A box will appear in the middle of the screen asking for what platform you would like to share on. Click the first option titled "Embed."

Step 3:

Another box will appear with the element used to display the video on your website in an html tag. Click "COPY" in the lower right hand corner to copy the line of html code onto your clipboard.

Step 4:

Paste the copied line of code into the body section of your html document for your webpage by right clicking the location and selecting "Paste."
